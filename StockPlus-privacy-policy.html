StockPulse Global — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

StockPulse Global (“the app”) is a portfolio tracker built around a simple principle: your financial data belongs on your device, not on our servers.

What we store, and where

  • Your portfolios, transactions, dividends, watchlists, alerts and settings are stored only on your device. They are never uploaded to us. We have no servers that hold your portfolio, and no way to see it.
  • You can export your data at any time (Settings → Export) and delete it at any time by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app.

What leaves your device

  • Ticker symbols only. To show prices, the app sends the stock/crypto symbols you track (for example “AAPL” or “BTC-USD”) to our price service, which fetches market quotes. These requests contain no personal information, no account identifiers, and no amounts — only the symbols and your device’s IP address, which is used solely to deliver the response and is not logged for tracking.
  • Nothing else. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party data collection.

Subscriptions

The app’s subscription is processed entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Billing, renewal and refunds are handled by the store under its own terms and privacy policy.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if an optional cloud-sync feature is added), the updated policy will be published at this address and the “Last updated” date will change. Features that involve any data leaving your device will always be optional and clearly labelled.
