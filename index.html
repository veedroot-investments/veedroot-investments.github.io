About

Veedroot Investments Trust is a Sydney-based discretionary investment trust. Alongside its investment activity, it develops and publishes independent software under the Veedroot name.

Every app is designed, built and maintained in-house — practical tools drawn from real work in transport and markets, rather than a catalogue of clones. The same care that goes into the investing goes into the software.
